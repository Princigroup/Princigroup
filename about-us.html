The Spheres of Society

Religion
In our mission, we understand religion as the spiritual foundation guiding all aspects of life, aiming for a positive impact on society through Christian principles.
Family
We encourage strengthening the family as the fundamental unit of society, promoting values that create a harmonious and healthy environment.
Government
Through our projects, we support the transformation of governments towards justice, equity, and transparency, influenced by the principles of the Gospel.
Education
We believe education is key to developing individuals who are both academically and ethically grounded.
Economy
We promote the creation of economic models that generate well-being and prosperity in alignment with Christian principles of generosity, honesty, and justice.
Art and Culture
We aim to spread art and culture that highlights Christian values, creating a deep connection with people through creativity.
Media
We strive to transform the media into channels that promote truth, justice, and peace, influenced by Gospel values.
