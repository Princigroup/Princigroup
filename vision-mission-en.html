Our Vision

At Princi & Co, we declare that by the year 2030, we will be a vessel of the Lord, operating as His subprovider on earth. We are dedicated to transforming the seven spheres of society: economy, government, family, education, arts, communication, and religion, with excellence in leadership, poverty alleviation, and supporting God’s mission worldwide.

Our vision is to empower leaders, communities, and organizations by providing biblically grounded training, consulting, and resources. We aim to spread the Gospel in Spain, Switzerland, France, and England, fulfilling the Great Commission and acting as a channel for radical transformation in leadership and missions. Through all our endeavors, we consistently apply God’s Word, storing treasures in heaven and managing our earthly resources with wisdom and purpose to glorify God and bring about spiritual and social change.

Our Mission

Princi & Co is dedicated to impacting the seven spheres of society—economy, government, family, education, arts, communication, and religion—by operating as God’s instruments of transformation. Our mission is to equip and empower leaders, organizations, and communities through biblically grounded training and resources, and to help fulfill their divine purpose while expanding God’s kingdom on earth.

As a beacon of hope, we actively engage in poverty alleviation and uplifting communities by reflecting God’s love and grace through our initiatives. We proclaim the Gospel boldly in Spain, Switzerland, France, and England, spreading the message of salvation to transform hearts and lives with the truth of Jesus Christ.

Princi & Co is committed to catalyzing radical change in leadership and missions by faithfully applying God’s Word in every aspect of our work. We store treasures in heaven, wisely using the resources God has entrusted to us to support missions and glorify Him in all that we do.

Our mission is to help establish Jesus’ reign in the hearts of individuals, bringing about spiritual, emotional, and societal transformation. We pray for God's guidance and strength to fulfill this mission, always giving glory, honor, and praise to Him.
